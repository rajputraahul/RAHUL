What is Mahadeva 

  Mahadeva is a Sanskrit term meaning "greatest god," "most powerful god" or "most supreme god."
  It is also the name sometimes given to the Hindu god, Shiva.
  Shiva is regarded by many as the first yogi, or the guru of yoga.
  It is said that he taught yoga to the ancient rishis for the good of all mankind.
